     <p><b>Renishaw plc</b> (<a href="/wiki/London_Stock_Exchange" title="London Stock Exchange">LSE</a>:&nbsp;<a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.londonstockexchange.com/exchange/searchengine/search.html?q=RSW">RSW</a>) is a British <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">engineering</a> company based in <a href="/wiki/Wotton-under-Edge" title="Wotton-under-Edge">Wotton-under-Edge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gloucestershire" title="Gloucestershire">Gloucestershire</a>. It is listed on the <a href="/wiki/London_Stock_Exchange" title="London Stock Exchange">London Stock Exchange</a> and is a constituent of the <a href="/wiki/FTSE_250_Index" title="FTSE 250 Index">FTSE 250 Index</a>.</p>

     <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renishaw_plc&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
     <p>The Company was founded by Sir <a href="/wiki/David_McMurtry" title="David McMurtry">David McMurtry</a> and John Deer in 1973.<sup id="cite_ref-history_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-history-2">[2]</a></sup> McMurtry had needed to measure fuel pipes on a prototype jet <a href="/wiki/Engine" title="Engine">engine</a>: at the time, <a href="/wiki/Coordinate-measuring_machine" title="Coordinate-measuring machine">coordinate-measuring machine</a> sensors featured rigid styli, which required manual positioning on the surface and which yielded poor repeatability when measuring delicate components. To meet this need, McMurtry invented a touch-trigger probe device,<sup id="cite_ref-history_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-history-2">[2]</a></sup> which he then <a href="/wiki/Patent" title="Patent">patented</a>. The probe featured an elegant 'kinematic' location for a spring-loaded stylus, providing a highly repeatable seated position for the stylus combined with the compliance needed to measure such components.</p> 
     <p>Renishaw was first listed on the <a href="/wiki/London_Stock_Exchange" title="London Stock Exchange">London Stock Exchange</a> in 1983.</p> 
     <p>In 2006 the Company bought 'itp', a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> manufacturer of precision styli.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p> 
     <p>In early 2009 the <a href="/wiki/Late_2000s_recession_in_Europe" title="Late 2000s recession in Europe" class="mw-redirect">global recession</a> reached Renishaw, resulting in a large proportion of the workforce being placed "at risk".<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p> 
     <p>Since 2009, Renishaw has seen a turnaround in business, with orders doubling within a year,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and the company stating that it had "recovered strongly since the worldwide recession".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p> 
     <p>In 2010 Renishaw bought a stake (and subsequently took complete control) of Measuring Devices Ltd, a company providing a range of services in the field of surveying equipment.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p> 
     <p>In 2011 Renishaw purchased the 400,000 sq ft <a href="/wiki/Robert_Bosch_GmbH" title="Robert Bosch GmbH">Bosch</a> plant in <a href="/wiki/Miskin" title="Miskin">Miskin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p> 
     <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Operations">Operations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renishaw_plc&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Operations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
     <p>Renishaw specialises in industrial <a href="/wiki/Metrology" title="Metrology">metrology</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spectroscopy" title="Spectroscopy">spectroscopy</a> and sells its products in the major markets around the world.</p> 
     <p>The company's strategy has been to grow organically sustained by patented innovations. The company's product portfolio has since expanded into touch probes for <a href="/wiki/CNC" title="CNC" class="mw-redirect">CNC</a> machine tools, <a href="/wiki/Calibration" title="Calibration">calibration</a> systems that optimise the performance of CNC machinery, <a href="/wiki/Linear_encoder" title="Linear encoder">linear encoder</a> systems, <a href="/wiki/Rotary_encoder" title="Rotary encoder">rotary encoder</a> systems, dental <a href="/wiki/CAD/CAM" title="CAD/CAM" class="mw-redirect">CAD/CAM</a> systems, <a href="/wiki/Raman_spectroscopy" title="Raman spectroscopy">Raman spectroscopy</a> and medical devices for functional <a href="/wiki/Neurosurgery" title="Neurosurgery">neurosurgery</a> applications.</p> 
     <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Locations">Locations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renishaw_plc&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Locations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
     <p>Renishaw's main offices are situated in an old <a href="/wiki/Mill_(factory)" title="Mill (factory)" class="mw-redirect">mill</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Industrial_revolution" title="Industrial revolution" class="mw-redirect">industrial revolution</a>, with several new buildings on a 26-acre (110,000&nbsp;m<sup>2</sup>) site. It is a <a href="/wiki/Nature" title="Nature">nature</a> oriented site, with dozens of <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">birds</a> (including <a href="/wiki/Duck" title="Duck">ducks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Peacock" title="Peacock" class="mw-redirect">peacocks</a>) and a large <a href="/wiki/Lake" title="Lake">lake</a>. The site is also close to the village of <a href="/wiki/Charfield" title="Charfield">Charfield</a>.</p> 
     <p>The company has a machine shop located at <a href="/wiki/Stonehouse,_Gloucestershire" title="Stonehouse, Gloucestershire">Stonehouse, Gloucestershire</a>, and an assembly facility at <a href="/wiki/Woodchester" title="Woodchester">Woodchester</a>, both near Stroud. There are further assembly facilities in <a href="/wiki/Dublin" title="Dublin">Dublin</a> (<a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Pune" title="Pune">Pune</a> (<a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>). Renishaw also has research facilities located in <a href="/wiki/Wotton-under-Edge" title="Wotton-under-Edge">Wotton-under-Edge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Edinburgh" title="Edinburgh">Edinburgh</a>, <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen" title="Aberdeen">Aberdeen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Exeter" title="Exeter">Exeter</a>.</p>
