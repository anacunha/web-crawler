     <p><b>Silesian University of Technology</b> (<a href="/wiki/Polish_language" title="Polish language">Polish</a> name: <b>Politechnika Śląska</b> IPA: [pɔliˈtɛxɲika ˈɕlɔ̃ska]) is a <a href="/wiki/University" title="University">university</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Gliwice" title="Gliwice">Gliwice</a>, <a href="/wiki/Silesia" title="Silesia">Silesia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a>. It was founded in 1945 by Polish professors of the <a href="/wiki/Lviv_University_of_Technology" title="Lviv University of Technology" class="mw-redirect">Lwow Polytechnic</a>, who were forced to leave their native city and move to the <a href="/wiki/Recovered_Territories" title="Recovered Territories">Recovered Territories</a> (see also <a href="/wiki/Kresy" title="Kresy">Kresy</a>). The University consistently ranks among the top most prestigious technical universities in Poland.</p> 
     <p>The Silesian University of Technology has 12 faculties:</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Architecture" title="Architecture">Architecture</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Automatic_control" title="Automatic control">Automatic control</a>, <a href="/wiki/Electronics" title="Electronics">Electronics</a> and <a href="/wiki/Computer_Science" title="Computer Science" class="mw-redirect">Computer Science</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Civil_Engineering" title="Civil Engineering" class="mw-redirect">Civil Engineering</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">Chemistry</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Electrical_Engineering" title="Electrical Engineering" class="mw-redirect">Electrical Engineering</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Philology" title="Philology">Philology</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Mining" title="Mining">Mining</a> and <a href="/wiki/Geology" title="Geology">Geology</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Energy" title="Energy">Energy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Environmental_Engineering" title="Environmental Engineering" class="mw-redirect">Environmental Engineering</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Applied_Mathematics" title="Applied Mathematics" class="mw-redirect">Applied Mathematics</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Mechanical_Engineering" title="Mechanical Engineering" class="mw-redirect">Mechanical Engineering</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Materials_Science" title="Materials Science" class="mw-redirect">Materials Science</a>, <a href="/wiki/Metallurgy" title="Metallurgy">Metallurgy</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Organisation" title="Organisation" class="mw-redirect">Organisation</a> and <a href="/wiki/Management" title="Management">Management</a>,</li> 
      <li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Transport" title="Transport">Transport</a>.</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p>The first nine faculties are situated in <a href="/wiki/Gliwice" title="Gliwice">Gliwice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rybnik" title="Rybnik">Rybnik</a> and the remaining three are in <a href="/wiki/Katowice" title="Katowice">Katowice</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zabrze" title="Zabrze">Zabrze</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tychy" title="Tychy">Tychy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bytom" title="Bytom">Bytom</a>.</p>
