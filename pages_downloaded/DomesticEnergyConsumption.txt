     <p><b>Domestic <a href="/wiki/Energy_consumption" title="Energy consumption">energy consumption</a></b> or <b>domestic consumption of energy</b> is the amount of energy that is spent on the various appliances used within housing. The amount of energy used per household varies widely depending on the standard of living of the country, climate, and the age and type of residence.</p> 
     <p>In the United States as of 2008, in an average household in a temperate climate, the yearly use of household energy can be composed as follows:</p>

     <p>This equates to an average instantaneous power consumption of 2&nbsp;kW at any given time.</p> 
     <p>Households in different parts of the world will have differing levels of consumption, based on climate and income.</p>
