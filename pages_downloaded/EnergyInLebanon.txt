     <p><b>Energy in Lebanon</b> describes <a href="/wiki/World_energy_resources_and_consumption" title="World energy resources and consumption" class="mw-redirect">energy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Electricity_generation" title="Electricity generation">electricity</a> production, consumption and import in <a href="/wiki/Lebanon" title="Lebanon">Lebanon</a>. <a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_policy_of_Lebanon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Energy policy of Lebanon (page does not exist)">Energy policy of Lebanon</a> will describe the <a href="/wiki/Energy_policy" title="Energy policy">energy policy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Politics_of_Lebanon" title="Politics of Lebanon">politics of Lebanon</a> more in detail. Lebanon imports most of its energy.</p> 
     <p>Primary energy use in 2009 in Lebanon was 77 TWh and 18 TWh per million persons.<sup id="cite_ref-IEA2010_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IEA2010-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> Primary energy use in Lebanon was 61 TWh and 15 TWh/million persons in 2008.<sup id="cite_ref-IEA2010_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IEA2010-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

     <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Gas">Gas</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_in_Lebanon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Gas">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
     <p>The <a href="/wiki/Arab_Gas_Pipeline" title="Arab Gas Pipeline">Arab Gas Pipeline</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Natural_gas" title="Natural gas">natural gas</a> pipeline exporting <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egyptian</a> natural gas to <a href="/wiki/Jordan" title="Jordan">Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Syria" title="Syria">Syria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lebanon" title="Lebanon">Lebanon</a>, with a separate line to <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>.</p> 
     <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Renewable_energy">Renewable energy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_in_Lebanon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Renewable energy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
     <p>Lebanese government intend to meet 12 percent of its total energy needs from renewable energy sources by 2020.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
